How to gamify your life

LevelUp is a personal assistant app that keeps track of tasks/goals/habits and helps you stay on top of them through gamification - essentially turning your life into an RPG where you build stats like strength and intelligence through accomplishing real life tasks like exercise and reading.

How it works

User registers for an account. User logins and adds either routines or task or both. The user is assigned basic starting stats is several different categories such as strength, vitality, intelligence and charisma

When the user completes task they receive experience points based on the type of categories selected and receives experience based on various parameters selected such as if the task in catagorized as either trivial or difficult.

Routine vs. Tasks

A routine is a parameter that is an ongoing goal that you set. These will usually consist of habits that you are either looking to accomplish on a scheduled basis. In addition you can set habits that you wish to discontinue. (i.e smoking)

A task is a short-term goal that has a once it's completed it is removed from your profile.
